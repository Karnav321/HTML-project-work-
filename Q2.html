Jaguars are powerful, solitary big cats, the largest in the Americas and third-largest globally, known for their distinctive rosette spots and strong jaws that can bite through skulls and shells. They are strong swimmers and climbers, occupying rainforests and wetlands, and are opportunistic carnivores with a diet that includes deer, fish, and even caiman. Though once more widespread, jaguars are now primarily found in Central and South America, classified as "near threatened" due to habitat loss and other threats.

The company emphasizes sustainability, operates globally with numerous production sites and a vast sales network, and has a significant history in motorsports. The B-2 Spirit is a US stealth flying-wing bomber designed for penetrating enemy airspace, using a combination of radar-absorbent materials and a smooth, angular design to avoid radar detection. It is capable of carrying both conventional and nuclear weapons, including the GBU-57A/B Massive Ordnance Penetrator (MOP), a bunker-buster bomb. The aircraft has a global range with aerial refueling, a payload capacity of over 40,000 pounds, and a two-pilot crew.
